Why we're building this

Two years ago, our founder was shipping a side project and needed a waitlist page — fast. Every tool she tried was either bloated with features she didn't need or locked the basics behind a paywall. So she built her own in a weekend.

Word got around. Other founders wanted the same thing: something clean, honest, and fast to set up. That weekend project is now becoming ProductLaunch — a home for makers who want to validate an idea and start building an audience before they've built the product.

What we're building

  • → A one-page site builder made just for launches
  • → Simple, spam-free email collection
  • → No-code setup — live in minutes, not weeks
  • → Honest pricing, built for solo founders
JD

The person behind it

Hi, I'm Jamie Davis — a product designer and self-taught developer who has launched (and quietly shelved) more side projects than I can count. Each one taught me the same lesson: momentum comes from an audience, and an audience starts with a single email address.

I'm building ProductLaunch full-time now, with a small crew of designers and engineers who believe launching shouldn't require a marketing team, a big budget, or six weeks of setup. Just an idea worth sharing.

Our mission

Give every maker the tools to launch fearlessly — and the confidence that comes from knowing people are already waiting to hear from them.
